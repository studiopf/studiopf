Studio PF creates high-end miniature paintings for collectors, enthusiasts and competitors looking to transform their models into true display pieces.

Every project is fully customized according to the miniature, its universe and its purpose: enriching a collection, displaying it in a showcase, or entering a painting competition.

These projects benefit from extensive artistic work that can represent dozens or even hundreds of hours of painting, using advanced techniques, meticulous attention to detail, and a constant pursuit of the highest possible quality.

I love creating unique collector pieces: Busts, Pop Culture Miniatures and Dioramas, fully customized for collectors, display purposes and painting competitions.

I paint busts, miniature statues and collector pieces designed for display cabinets or to enhance private collections.

Every project receives a fully personalized approach to highlight the sculpt, volumes, textures and character expression. Skin tones, metals, leather, fabrics, weathering and atmosphere effects: every detail is carefully crafted to bring the miniature to life.

These projects are intended for both collectors and enthusiasts looking for a unique piece, whether it is a historical bust, fantasy, science-fiction or an original creation.

Do you own a miniature inspired by your favorite universe? I also paint models from manga, movies, TV series, video games and pop culture licenses, whether they feature iconic characters or original creations.

Dragon Ball, One Piece, Naruto, Demon Slayer, Star Wars, Marvel, DC Comics, The Lord of the Rings, The Witcher, Elden Ring, Zelda, Warcraft... All licenses are welcome.

Most of these models come from independent 3D artists or are available through specialized platforms such as Etsy, MyMiniFactory and other collector-oriented stores.

Important Information

Studio PF does not provide miniatures and does not offer 3D printing as part of this service.
The client supplies the miniature or purchases it directly from the creator or seller of their choice. Studio PF then prepares, paints and enhances the model with a premium-quality finish.
