Do you lack the time to paint your army, want a more consistent finish, or simply wish to play with miniatures you can be proud of?

Studio PF transforms your miniatures into a cohesive, readable and spectacular army on the gaming table. Every project is hand-painted according to your colours, your universe and the level of finish you want.

Two painting levels are available:

The Gold level is designed for armies, units and large quantities of miniatures.

You get a clean, consistent and immediately readable result in play, without paying for details that are barely visible at gaming distance.

What is included:
main colours, shading, targeted highlights, essential details, unit consistency and a clean finish.

Estimated time per miniature:
approximately 30 minutes to 1 hour depending on size, complexity and chosen colours.
